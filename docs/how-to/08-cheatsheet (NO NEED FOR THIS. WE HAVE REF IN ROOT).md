## рџљ¦ The "Memory" Meter

Watch the `Ctx(u):` (Context Usage) at the bottom of your screen.

- **< 50%**: You're golden.
- **50вЂ“70%**: Be specific. Don't load huge files if you don't need them.
- **70вЂ“90%**: Run `/compact` to summarize the chat and free up space.
- **> 90%**: High risk of the AI getting "hallucinations." Run `/clear`.

## рџ’Ў Better Prompting

When you ask for something, follow this 4-step mental check:

1. **WHAT**: Precisely what do you want? (e.g., "A summary of...")
2. **WHERE**: Which files should I look at? (Use `@`)
3. **HOW**: Any specific style or rules?
4. **VERIFY**: How will we know it's right?
