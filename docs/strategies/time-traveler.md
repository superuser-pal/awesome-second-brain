# Time Traveler Council

## Strategy

Convene three voices for the decision at hand: (1) **Past-you** — what relevant experience, mistakes, or patterns from your history apply here? (2) **Present-you** — what are the current pressures, constraints, and instincts? (3) **Future-you** — looking back from 12 months ahead, what would you wish you had done or avoided? Weigh all three, note any tension, and return the integrated recommendation.

## When to apply

- Long-term decisions where short-term pressure is distorting judgment.
- Weekly or monthly reviews where historical patterns should inform future plans.
- Career, project, or priority decisions with compounding consequences.
- Any time "what feels urgent now" and "what matters long-term" are in conflict.

## Trade-offs

- **Pro**: Counteracts recency bias and short-term urgency — grounds decisions in continuity.
- **Pro**: The "future-you" voice is particularly powerful for surfacing regret-avoidance.
- **Con**: Past and future voices are projections — quality depends on honest self-knowledge.
- **Con**: Can feel introspective rather than analytical; may not suit purely technical decisions.
